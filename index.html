Project Idea

Tourism Planning Application

Overview

My project idea is called ‘Tourism Planning Application’, and I plan to make a travel planning app. The main function of the app is to plan the most convenient and time-saving routes by selecting the tourist attractions, tourist areas or locations that tourists want to visit (the location can be one or more).

Most importantly, the app will not only plan the route, but also recommend accommodation places to stay on that route that meet the tourists' needs (such as convenient transportation, the most time-saving travel, the most delicious food, etc.), food locations, and the most convenient transportation strategy.

Motivation

Nowadays, tourism is one of the largest industries in the world, with an estimated annual worldwide contribution is nearly $7 trillion. Recently, more and more people like traveling and many choose to take a short or long trip during their holiday. Maps or navigation can only solve the route problem, not directly give the most convenient and high-quality itinerary planning for the whole trip.

Most people only have an idea of where they want to go and how they decide to go, but they have no idea about the route planning and the choice of accommodation and food, some people even feel distressed because they have to compare and choose the most convenient and the best accommodation on the route between places of interest.

Description

  • Features:

  • There are more routes for you to choose, so you don't have to be stuck with one route or mode of travel. Convenient routes can be generated just by your click

  • The corresponding costs are reduced and you don't have to spend money for unnecessary things. For example, in route planning, a route that does not go your way will waste some time and transportation costs. What’s more, you can save the cost of hiring professionals to help with planning.

  • Saving time cost in a significant way, you can greatly reduce the time you spent on planning your trip and really leave as soon as you want to. Then, you don’t need to spend a lot of time on wrong or unfamiliar routes.

  • Cost-effective. In terms of price, it saves people the expense of joining a group tour or having a travel agent help them plan the route in a significant way. In terms of itinerary, comparing to signing up for group tours, my app is more suitable for young people who want to travel unrestrained and freely.

  • There is more freedom for travelers.

  • You can decide your own itinerary and route. Accommodation, food and transportation can be filtered by your preference level.

  • Avoiding the formation of boring trips and make people look forward to traveling.

  • Improving the happiness index of the whole journey.

  • Functions:

    • Planning Routes & Recommendations For Accommodation and Food

    Before traveling, most people only have the destination they want to go to, but are confused about the route planning, places to stay, food recommendations and transportation conditions. Many people will spend a lot of time in advance just to make a travel guide, which is a headache for many people who do not have that enough time before traveling. There are also most people who choose not to go out or travel because they don't want to spend a lot of time and effort on planning their route and making choices.

    In order to avoid this situation, I want to create an App with the function of route planning, accommodation and food recommendations. People only need to enter or select the attractions they want to go to (it can be on or more), and then choose an acceptable way of transportation, such as self-driving tours, public transportation, etc. Waiting for a while, you can get several planned routes. Not only that, when people choose a favorite route, the App will recommend accommodation and food places suitable for the route, and people can clearly understand all the appropriate information on this route.Most importantly, the routes recommended by the app must be safe and fast in order to ensure the safety of tourists who traveling alone.Of course, while giving recommendations for accommodation and food, the app will also give the most suitable transportation mode on the route and people can filter according to their preferences.

    Being different from ordinary map, navigation and other software, maps and navigation will only provide driving or traffic routes, and will not provide more convenient and quality places to stay and eat on this route. With this app, you can achieve such a function.
